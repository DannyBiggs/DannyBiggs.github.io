⌨️ Commands

!forge
Pull 1 coin from the Coin Forge.
!forge 5
Pull multiple coins, up to 5, if you have enough Resonance.
!forge gamble
Spend full Resonance for a risky gamble pull.
!resonance
Check your current Forge Resonance.
!materials
View your Forge materials.
!stats
View your Coin Forge profile, coins, XP, and progression.
!title
View or equip unlocked nameplate titles.
!coinshop
View boosts, effects, and special shop items.
!leaderboardweekly
View the weekly Coin Forge leaderboard.
