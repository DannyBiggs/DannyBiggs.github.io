📖 How to Play

1. Pull Coins
Use !forge to pull coins from the Coin Forge.
2. Earn Rewards
Coins grant currency, XP, progress, achievements, badges, and titles.
3. Spend Resonance
Each pull uses Forge Resonance. Use !resonance to check yours.
4. Equip Cosmetics
Unlock nameplates and badges, then show them off during pulls.

🧭 Progression Roadmap

Start Here
Use !forge and learn the coin tiers.
Build Resonance
Manage your pull resource and check it with !resonance.
Unlock Achievements
Pull rare coins, gamble, fight bosses, and trigger forge events.
Equip Cosmetics
Earn badges and nameplate titles to show off during pulls.
Fight Bosses
Avaros and Invidra twist the forge when greed and envy rise.

🪙 Coin Guide
Wood Coin
Forge Effect: Adds fuel to the Burn Pile.
Why it matters: Helps trigger Forge Blessings and Forge Ignites events.
Every great flame begins with something humble enough to burn.
Bronze Coin
Forge Effect: A small but safe reward.
Why it matters: Builds early coins, XP, and achievement progress.
Not every pull is legendary. Some are the foundation legends stand on.
Silver Coin
Forge Effect: A reliable mid-tier payout.
Why it matters: Starts pushing Envy higher when no boss is active.
A clean strike. A bright spark. The forge begins to notice.
Gold Coin
Forge Effect: A strong pull worth chasing.
Why it matters: Progresses Gold achievements, Gilded rewards, and Envy pressure.
Fortune rings loudest when gold hits the anvil.
Diamond Coin
Forge Effect: A rare high-value treasure.
Why it matters: Drives Diamond milestones and heavily feeds Envy pressure.
A flawless shard of luck, struck from impossible heat.
Rainbow Coin
Forge Effect: The forge’s mythic jackpot-tier blessing.
Why it matters: Unlocks Rainbow progression and causes a major Envy spike.
The flame breaks into colors it was never meant to show.
Anti Coin
Forge Effect: A cursed pull that punishes the user.
Why it matters: Breaks momentum, progresses curse achievements, and can heal active bosses.
The forge gives. The forge takes. Sometimes it takes first.
Lucky Coin
Forge Effect: Improves or rescues the next pull.
Why it matters: Can turn weak outcomes into something worth celebrating.
A coin that lands on its edge and dares fate to argue.
Jackpot Booster
Forge Effect: Feeds the shared jackpot pool.
Why it matters: Makes future gamble outcomes more explosive.
The pot grows heavier. So does the temptation.
Kingmaker Coin
Forge Effect: A premium fortune coin tied to powerful outcomes.
Why it matters: Designed for rare, memorable, stream-defining moments.
Some coins buy power. This one crowns it.

🛒 Shop / Boosts
⚡ XP Boost
Boosts XP gained from upcoming positive coin pulls.
💰 Double Value
Doubles the value of upcoming positive coin pulls.
🛡️ Anti Protection
Protects against an Anti Coin by converting it into a safer outcome.
🎯 Force Tier
Improves the next coin outcome by forcing or upgrading its tier.
🍀 Lucky Coin
Improves the next pull and can convert low-value outcomes upward.
🎟️ Golden Ticket
Forces a Gold+ style pull when activated.
🎰 Jackpot Booster
Adds value to the shared jackpot pool.
👑 Kingmaker
A premium special coin tied to powerful fortune outcomes.
⌨️ Commands
!forge
Pull 1 coin from the Coin Forge.
!forge 5
Pull multiple coins, up to 5, if you have enough Resonance.
!forge gamble
Spend full Resonance for a risky gamble pull.
!resonance
Check your current Forge Resonance.
!materials
View your Forge materials.
!stats
View your Coin Forge profile, coins, XP, and progression.
!title
View or equip unlocked nameplate titles.
!coinshop
View boosts, effects, and special shop items.
!leaderboardweekly
View the weekly Coin Forge leaderboard.
