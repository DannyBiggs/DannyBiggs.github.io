📖 How to Play

1. Pull Coins
Use !forge to pull coins from the Coin Forge.
2. Earn Rewards
Coins grant currency, XP, progress, achievements, badges, and titles.
3. Spend Resonance
Each pull uses Forge Resonance. Use !resonance to check yours.
4. Equip Cosmetics
Unlock nameplates and badges, then show them off during pulls.

🧭 Progression Roadmap

🪙 Coin Guide
⚪ Wood Coin
Forge Effect: Adds fuel to the Burn Pile.
Why it matters: Helps trigger Forge Blessings and Forge Ignites events.
⚪ Bronze Coin
Forge Effect: A small but safe reward.
Why it matters: Builds early coins, XP, and achievement progress.
🔵 Silver Coin
Forge Effect: A reliable mid-tier payout.
Why it matters: Starts pushing Envy higher when no boss is active.
🟣 Gold Coin
Forge Effect: A strong pull worth chasing.
Why it matters: Progresses Gold achievements, Gilded rewards, and Envy pressure.
🟠 Diamond Coin
Forge Effect: A rare high-value treasure.
Why it matters: Drives Diamond milestones and heavily feeds Envy pressure.
🌈 Rainbow Coin
Forge Effect: The forge’s mythic jackpot-tier blessing.
Why it matters: Unlocks Rainbow progression and causes a major Envy spike.
💀 Anti Coin
Forge Effect: A cursed pull that punishes the user.
Why it matters: Breaks momentum, progresses curse achievements, and can heal active bosses.
